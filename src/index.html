What is SolEstate DAO

SolEstate is an NFT real estate project investment vehicle fusing the Metaverse through real-world property ownership utilizing NFT fractionalization. SolEstate.land Decentralised Autonomous Organisation (DAO) is a Real property estate community experiment, based and built on the Solana blockchain.

We resolved to build on the Solana blockchain because it processes transactions at an exceedingly faster rate than the Ethereum blockchain with about 50,000 transactions per second. This speed of transaction translates to lower costs of transaction which is critical for user experience as high transaction fees can act as a hindrance to an entry point in the market.

This time we are redefining the NFT narative

NFTs have limitless potentials however, the NFT market is currently crammed with art-based NFTs. Art NFTs in itself isn’t a bad thing, nonetheless, there is evidence of severe devaluations mere minutes after minting these. The reason is a simple case of higher supply versus lower demand as art NFTs are surplus in the market.

We had these aforementioned drawbacks of the existing NFTs in mind during our project creation phase. SolEstate provides its holders with the opportunity to be involved in real estate while owning art. SolEstate will be a collection of certificate NFTs that entitles its holder’s ownership in real landed properties both in the physical and the metaverse while serving as art simultaneously.

Ownership of a piece in the of Land here and on the Metaverse

SolEstate will be collections of certificates NFTs that serve as "ART" and connect the holder to real land ownership in the physical and metaverse. Each NFT represents 1/10000 ownership of resorts, hotels, and gold mines. In other words, each NFT in the collection will represent 1/10000th of a share in the ownership of each revenue-generating property.
